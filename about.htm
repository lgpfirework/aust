Introducing Studio

安小理工作室成立于2013年10月，是由安徽理工大学在校学子自发组成的一个工作室。

初期主要以学校微信平台的建设为主要任务，继而赢得了学校师生的支持与鼓励。随着工作室的发展，又在微信平台的基础上更上一层楼，推出了安徽理工大学首款校园APP“掌上安理”及“小理电台”“安理论坛微信平台”等栏目，在校园中深得同学们欢迎。2014年5月安小理工作室加入大学生新媒体发展研究社。

Anxiaoli Studio(A team) was founded in 2013 October, is a studio from Anhui University Of Science And Technology students spontaneously. At the early stage of construction, mainly by school wechat platform as the main task, and won the support and encouragement of teachers and students in the school. With the development of the studio, and wechat platform building on the Anhui University Of Science And Technology campus, launched the first APP "Aust in Hand" , "Xiaoli Radio ","Wechat Union" and other columns, the classmates welcome at the campus in. In 2014 May Anxiaoli Studio took part in the union for the study of new media development.

Introducing aust-helper
